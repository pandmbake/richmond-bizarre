The Byrd Theater

The Byrd Theater, located in Carytown, is reported to be the one of the most haunted buildings in Richmond. Opened in 1928, this movie-only theater features the "Mighty Wurlitzer" organ, originally designed to accompany silent films.

One theater manager said there are at least two ghosts inhabiting the building. One is a little girl who has often been seem playing in the women's bathroom. The other is the spirit of the former manager, Mr. Coulter, who worked at the theater between 1928 and 1971. He has been seen all over the theater, but is most often seen haunting the balcony section. The Byrd is another must visit Richmond location, nestled in the center of the very "hip" Carytown shopping district. For more info on the Byrd, check out their official website.
